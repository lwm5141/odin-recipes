Waffles

Description

Breakfast made the way your mom used to do it. These waffles will make you feel like a kid again and make your head fill with delicious memories of your youth.

These golden brown waffles will surely hit the sweet spot whether you plan on preparing them for your entire family or just for yourself to enjoy! (We won't judge)

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 4 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons of white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups of warm milk
  • 1/3 cup of butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, mix together flour, salt, baking powder and sugar; set aside. Preheat waffle iron to desired temperature.
  2. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs. Stir in the milk, butter and vanilla. Pour the milk mixture into the flour mixture; beat until blended.
  3. Ladle the batter into a preheated waffle iron. Cook the waffles until golden and crisp. Serve immediately.
